What is Réponse?

Réponse is a web based QnA service based on the Blockchain platform. Not only can you ask questions that you are curious about, but also answer questions that others have asked at Réponse. You receive points depending on the answers you have provided. You can switch the points (cryptocurrency) you earn to money. All answers and content on our Blockchain platform will be copyrighted and the ability to to change and abuse will not be allowed.

What types of questions can be asked?

Any and all questions can be asked. However, before asking your question we recommend that you first check to see if a similar question has already been asked within the community at Reponse. You can search the question by title or tags through the search function.

What types of questions should be avoided?

Questions deemed to be controversial or too subjective should be avoided.

What types of answers should be avoided?

Réponse is not a discussion service but a QnA service. We do not recommend disputable answers because too much discussion tends to dilute the essence of a QnA service. This is why controversial questions should be avoided.

Who moderates this community?

You. The community is arbitrated by Users. The Reponse point system allows Users to earn rights to perform a variety of arbitration tasks. (Our point system grants Users the rights to perform a variety of arbitration tasks.)

How does our point system work?

When a question or answer is upvoted by other Users, the User who posted the question or answer will gain points according to the predetermined point table. The sum of the points will be regarded as a standard to show the reliability of the Users. Various moderation tasks are gradually assigned to the Users based on the points accumulated.For example, if an interesting question or a helpful answer is posted by Users, the posted question or answer will be upvoted by other Users. The point table below shows the scale of reliability.
Upvote 100

Gold Badge 30,000

Silver Badge 10,000

Bronze badge 5,000

Accept own answer Setting Point

How and when the points can be changed to currency coins?

The minimum number of points required is 30,000 points. The minimum number of points is required to exchange the points to currency tokens. Additionally, 3 answers must be posted and the User must login for more than 10 days. The ability to exchange points to currency tokens will not be allowed if any of the aforementioned conditions are not met.If the conditions are met, Go to “my page” and use “exchange function” to change points to currency coin.p>/t

What is the value of points?

The value of the Réponse community point is 1,000 points = $1 = 20 Res coin. Upon listing on cryptocurrency exchanges, we will try to keep the value “1,000 points = $1 = 20 Res Coin as an alternative against currency volatility.

When will Res Coin commence trading on cryptocurrency exchanges?

It is expected that Res tokens will be listed in 3 or 4 Q of 2018. Currently under negotiation with four potential cryptocurrency exchanges.

Search before asking your questions
Type your question in the search bar and see whether a similar question has been asked previously.

Search options are as follows:

to search in title - enter [title: your text]
to search by tags - enter [tag: sometag] or #sometag
to search by user - enter [user: somename] or @somename or @"some name"
In addition, it is possible to click on tags to add them to the search query.

Important!!! All search terms which are combined with a logical “AND” expression can help narrow the search scope by adding new terms..
